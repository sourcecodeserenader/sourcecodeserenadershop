About

My journey from tinkerer to tech creative.

  • 1995

    Born in Battle Creek, MI

    From my first RadioShack kit to dismantling mom’s VCR to see how it worked, curiosity has always driven me. Even in grade school I bypassed class and snuck off to the library to devour BASIC programming books.

  • 2015

    Becoming a Mom

    When Isabella arrived, I discovered my greatest project yet: balancing the code editor with diapers and bedtime stories. Motherhood taught me patience, empathy, and a new type of resilience.

  • 2019–2021

    Three Little Girls

    Raising three daughters in three years was my ultimate multitasking bootcamp. Between ballet recitals, science fair projects, and coding deadlines, I learned how to prioritize, automate the small stuff, and celebrate every tiny victory.

  • 2022

    Studying CS at SNHU

    I dove into cybersecurity, software engineering, and system design. Late-night hackathons and team projects sharpened my skills, and I realized the power of collaboration in driving real-world impact.

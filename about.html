About Me

My journey from tinkerer to tech creative.

1995

Born in Battle Creek, MI. From my first RadioShack kit to dismantling mom’s VCR, curiosity drove me to devour every BASIC programming book in the school library.

2015

Becoming a mom taught me my greatest lesson: balance code editors with bedtime stories. Patience, empathy and resilience became part of my daily workflow.

2019–2021

Raising three daughters in three years was the ultimate multitasking bootcamp—between ballet recitals and hackathons, I automated the small stuff and celebrated each tiny victory.

2022

At SNHU I dove into cybersecurity, software engineering, and system design. Late-night hackathons and team projects sharpened my skills and taught me the power of collaboration.
